Shiv Jyoti International School

Welcome to Shiv Jyoti International School, a premier educational institution nestled in the beautiful city of Kota, Rajasthan. Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), we offer comprehensive integrated learning programs designed to nurture every aspect of our students' growth.

Our commitment is to foster a vibrant learning environment where creativity, curiosity, and critical thinking thrive. We believe in equipping our students with not only academic knowledge but also the essential life skills to succeed in an ever-changing world.

MISSION

Shiv Jyoti International School's mission is to provide a holistic learning experience that nurtures intellectual, emotional, and social development of our students. We strive to create an environment that fosters creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning. We aim to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to succeed in an ever-changing world.
