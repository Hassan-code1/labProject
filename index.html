Shiv Jyoti International School

Welcome to Shiv Jyoti International School, a premier educational institution located in the beautiful city of Kota, Rajasthan. Our school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers integrated learning programs that cater to the holistic development of our students.

MISSION

Shiv Jyoti International School's mission is to provide a holistic learning experience that nurtures intellectual, emotional, and social development of our students. We strive to create an environment that fosters creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning. We aim to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to succeed in an ever-changing world.
